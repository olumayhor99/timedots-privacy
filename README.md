# Privacy Policy – TimeDots

**Last updated:** March 2026

TimeDots respects your privacy. The app is designed to be simple, private, and fully offline. We do not collect, track, or share your personal data.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

TimeDots does **not** collect any personal information.

Specifically:
- No accounts
- No sign-ups
- No analytics
- No tracking
- No advertising identifiers
- No third-party SDKs
- No location data
- No usage data sent off your device

All calculations are performed locally on your device using Apple’s system frameworks.

---

## 2. In-App Purchases

TimeDots offers optional **one-time in-app purchases** to unlock additional features.

All payments are:
- Processed entirely by **Apple**
- Handled through the **App Store**
- Governed by Apple’s own privacy policy

TimeDots does **not** receive or store:
- Payment information
- Billing details
- Personal identifiers related to purchases

We only receive confirmation from Apple that a purchase has been completed.

---

## 3. Data Stored on Your Device

TimeDots may store the following **locally on your device only**:
- App preferences (e.g. selected theme or view mode)
- Purchase unlock status
- Optional user settings

This data is stored using Apple’s local storage mechanisms (such as `UserDefaults`) and **never leaves your device**.

---

## 4. How We Use Your Information

Because no personal data is collected:
- Nothing is analyzed
- Nothing is shared
- Nothing is sold
- Nothing is transmitted to external servers

TimeDots works entirely offline.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

TimeDots does **not** use:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting tools
- Cloud storage
- External APIs

The only third-party service involved is **Apple’s App Store** for processing in-app purchases.

---

## 6. Sharing & Exports

If you choose to export or share an image (e.g. a time visualization card), that action is fully controlled by you using Apple’s system share sheet. TimeDots does not access or track where you share content.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

TimeDots does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

---

## 8. Your Control

You have full control over your data:
- Deleting the app removes all locally stored data
- No data exists outside your device

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

If the app’s privacy practices ever change, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. Any changes will be reflected with an updated **Last updated** date.
